How it all Began

I picked photography from my dad who did semi-professional photography. As he had less and less time to pursue his hobby, I emerged with a great interest in photography and I inherited his camera as my first camera. He is not only the person that allowed to me to pursue this hobby, but he is also my mentor and my guide to further expand my knowledge. Photography has since then changed my view in the world. By not only capturing the worl has to offer in one picture, I have realized the world has so many beautiful things we under appreciate.
